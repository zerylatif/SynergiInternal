30 Day Plan

30 Days

Learning

Priorities: Get up to speed on your role, team, and Synergi Recruitment. Understand the expectations your Team Leader has of you, learn how the internal processes and procedures currently work, and start to explore some tools available to dive into your role.

60 Days

Contribution
